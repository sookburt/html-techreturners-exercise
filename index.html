Introduction

Tim Berners Lee dreamt of a World Wide Web that would be free and accessible to eveyone. Sadly, the modern web is littered with poorly designed websites and difficult to navigate code (both for developers and users). This often comes down to a lack of understanding around different user needs.

Designing pages that are accessible for everyone shouldn't be too difficult. The developers at Tech Returners News have collated an 'easy wins' checklist fof Web page accessibility.

Keep this guide handy... When creating your own websites you can check these criteria off the list!
